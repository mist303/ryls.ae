How it works

One query. Three outputs. Defensible record.

Submit a transaction's parameters — sender bank, receiver bank, currency, amount, deadline — and PTRI returns a structured routing view in seconds.

⚖️

Rail Availability Matrix

Instantly see which rails are open for a given sender–receiver–currency combination, based on live CIPS participant topology and bank-level configuration.

💰

Cost Comparison

Side-by-side fee and FX-spread breakdown for CIPS Direct, CIPS Indirect, and SWIFT correspondent paths. Transparent per-leg arithmetic, single rolled-up figure.

📋

Routing Decision Memo

A time-stamped, hash-chained PDF capturing the rail recommendation, cost inputs, sanctions-list versions, and screening source — ready for your compliance file.

🔗

Screening Orchestration

Connects to your existing screening vendor — ComplyAdvantage, Refinitiv WorldCheck, Dow Jones, and others — via a lightweight adapter. You stay in control of the verdict.

🏦

Bank-Topology Awareness

Knows which banks are CIPS Direct vs Indirect Participants, cross-referenced daily against major sanctions lists so unavailable rails are flagged before you commit.

🔒

Audit Trail

Every routing query and memo is hash-chained to an append-only audit log. A verification endpoint lets you confirm any PDF's authenticity on demand.
